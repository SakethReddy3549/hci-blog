My Interests


Human-Computer Interaction is fascinating because it sits at the crossroads of technology and human behavior. As someone with a background in working behind the scenes on data—managing databases, optimizing workflows, and integrating complex pipelines—I’ve always been curious about the why behind user needs. How do we make systems not just functional but truly intuitive? Through this class, I want to broaden my understanding of designing systems that serve users effectively, whether they’re navigating an interface, processing large datasets, or analyzing results. I’m particularly interested in learning how HCI principles can improve interactions for data-intensive platforms, ensuring usability and efficiency for those working with massive datasets.
